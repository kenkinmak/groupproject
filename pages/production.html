Field Production

Whether you are looking for a production crew to film an interview or document a live event, Kingston Media offers full production teams and equipment packages to produce media content in the field. Our production teams typically include from two to five person crews and can consist of a director, cinematographer, audio technician, lighting technician, and production assistant.

We offer broadcast quality, high-definition field production packages and film exclusively on P2 solid state memory cards so that we can immediately begin editing as soon as the director says cut.

Kingston Media has been creating video content for over a decade, working with large and small businesses, government agencies, and local organizations to convey messages through this medium. Our ability to entertain and educate while emotionally connecting with audiences separates us from other video production companies.

We make the production process quite fun and efficient. First, we have a casual, informal meeting to learn about your organization and discuss the goals of the project. We create an outline or script that reflects these objectives. We schedule the location and talent, and film with our complete high-definition production package. The magic is created at our production facility in our high-definition edit suite where we edit the story, add footage, music and motion graphics. Our goal on every project is to not only meet, but to exceed your expectations.

Studio Production

If you are looking for the convenience and relaxation of a production studio, Kingston Media offers a facility where you can come to us and have your message delivered at our fully equipped production studio. We can conduct an interview or load your message into our teleprompter so you can do a direct read to the camera. We also offer an in-house talent to make your infomercial or marketing piece come to life.

Post Production

Kingston Media offers fully equipped high-definition editing and audio production suites, as well as duplication services.
