About

Kingston Media is a San Francisco Bay Area video production company. For over 15 years, Kingston Media has created powerful stories to educate, entertain, and inspire. Located on the beautiful California Coast in San Mateo County, Kingston Media offers award-winning producers with the latest in technology. Whether you are looking to produce a video for broadcast, event, or the web, Kingston Media is your one-stop shop for all your video production needs. Kingston Media also offers videography for events, video editing and audio-visual services.

We are based in San Mateo County, California

We work with clients from all over the Bay Area. Get in touch with us!
